## Objective
Enhance the legal document generation system by introducing exclusive features for **Pro and Enterprise** users. Implement advanced AI capabilities to improve customization, compliance, automation, and user experience.

## Feature Enhancements

### 1️⃣ Clause Customization
- Allow users to dynamically **edit, reorder, and remove** suggested legal clauses while maintaining legal integrity.

### 2️⃣ Legal Language Refinement
- Provide options for different writing tones:  
  - **Formal** (strict legal language)  
  - **Simple** (easy-to-understand terms)  
  - **Industry-Specific** (customized legal jargon)  

### 3️⃣ AI-Powered Risk Analysis
- Highlight **potential legal risks** in generated documents and suggest safer alternatives.

### 4️⃣ Jurisdiction-Based Adaptation
- Modify legal terms based on **country-specific regulations** to ensure compliance with local laws.

### 5️⃣ Regulatory Updates
- Notify users about relevant **legal changes** affecting their documents.

### 6️⃣ Pre-filled Legal Templates
- Enable AI to **auto-fill** standard clauses based on user inputs for **faster** document creation.

### 7️⃣ Document Auto-Summarization
- Generate **simple, non-legal summaries** of complex agreements for easy understanding.

### 8️⃣ Multi-Language Support
- Provide **translations** of legal documents into **multiple languages** while preserving legal accuracy.

### 9️⃣ Electronic Signature Integration
- Allow users to **sign documents** within the platform using built-in **e-signature capabilities**.

### 10️⃣ Multi-User Collaboration
- Enable **team members** to review, comment, and edit documents in **real-time** for **Enterprise users**.

### 11️⃣ Bulk Document Generation
- Allow users to **create multiple agreements simultaneously** for efficiency.

### 12 API Access for Automation
- Provide **Enterprise users** with API access to **automate document generation** within their systems.

### 13 Priority Support & Expert Consultation
- Ensure **faster response times** and offer **AI-generated legal advice** with optional **human review**.
